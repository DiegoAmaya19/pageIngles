Future Simple

Explication

El futuro simple es un tiempo verbal que utilizamos para hablar de acciones que ocurrirán en el futuro, sin especificar un momento exacto. Es como decir "en algún momento en el futuro".

Estructuras

Estructura de oraciones afirmativas:

Pronombre + will + verbo pasado + Complemento

Estructura de oraciones Negativas:

Prononbre + will + not+ verbo Infinitivo + Complemento

Questions, affirmative and negative

Will you go shopping this weekend? Yes, I will. No, I won't. Yes, I will go shopping this weekend. I need to buy some new clothes. No, I won't go shopping this weekend. I'm too busy.
Will you study tonight? Yes, I will. No, I won't. Yes, I will study tonight. I have a test tomorrow. No, I won't study tonight. I'm too tired.
Will you cook dinner tomorrow? Yes, I will. No, I won't. Yes, I will cook dinner tomorrow. I'm in the mood for a homemade meal. No, I won't cook dinner tomorrow. I'll order takeout.
Will you travel next year? Yes, I will. No, I won't. Yes, I will travel next year. I'm planning a trip to Europe. No, I won't travel next year. I need to save money.
