Present Simple

Explication

El presente simple es un tiempo en ingles usado principalmente para representar el presente de forma simplificado.

Estructuras

Estructura de oraciones afirmativas:

Prononbre + verbo Infinitivo + Complemento

Estructura de oraciones Negativas:

Prononbre + aux + not+ verbo Infinitivo + Complemento

Reglas de ES, IES y S

When the verb ends in: he, she, it

Sh, ch, z, o, x, s. -> ES

She washes He goes She fixes

Consonant + y IES

Study -> Studies Carry -> Carries

The rest of the verb S

Exercise

Every morning, Sarah wakes up at 6:00 a.m. and immediately prepares a healthy breakfast for her family. She makes fresh orange juice by squeezing several oranges, toasts whole wheat bread, and scrambles eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Afterward, she sets the table neatly, places all the food on it, and calls her children to come downstairs for breakfast. While they eat together, Sarah talks about the plans for the day and ensures that everyone is ready for their respective activities. Once breakfast is over, she cleans the kitchen, washes the dishes, and begins her day with a sense of accomplishment.

I am immediately a good person -> Soy inmediatamente una Buena persona I immediately prepare breakfast -> Preparo inmediatamente el desayuno I am healthy -> Soy saludable She makes fresh orange juice by squeezing several oranges -> Ella hace jugo de naranja fresco exprimiendo varias naranjas. Toasts whole wheat bread -> Tostadas de pan integral Wheat -> trigo / Whole -> entero o integral / bread -> pan scrambles eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper -> Huevos revueltos con una pisa de sal y pimienta She sets the table neatly -> Ella pone la mesa ordenadamente Places -> lugares / poner (she places the book on the table) Calls her children to come downstairs for breakfast -> llama a sus hijos para que bajen a desayunar (come downstairs -> vengan a bajo) ensures that everyone is ready for their respective activities -> garantiza que todos estén listos para sus respectivas actividades. Once breakfast is over -> Una vez terminado el desayuno Every -> cada uno, o todos (objetos) -> Every book in the house She cleans the kitchen -> Ella limpio la cocina. Washes the dishes -> Lava los platos She begins her day with a sense of accomplishment -> Ella comenzo su dia con una sensacion de logro.
